Ballhandler states 

going_up 
	Flipping ball handler up (not spinning)
	Caused by hitting up button on right stick  

going_down
	Flipping ball handler down (not spinning) 
	Caused by hitting down button on the right stick 

down_out_on
	Handler flipped down, spinning and out

down_in_off
	handler flipped down, not spinning and in

up_if_off 
	Handler flipped up and not spinning 
	Caused by hitting a button on the right stick or automatically by ball sensor
